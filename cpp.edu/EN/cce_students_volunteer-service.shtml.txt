                                            <h2>Civic Engagement and Voter Empowerment</h2>
<p><a href="https://nationalvoterregistrationday.org/get-voteready/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">National Voter Registration Day</a> is a nonpartisan civic holiday celebrating our democracy. Over 5 million voters have registered to vote on the holiday to date. According to the U.S. Census data from 2020, as many as 1 in 4 eligible Americans are not registered to vote. Every year, millions of Americans find themselves unable to vote because they miss a registration deadline, don&#8217;t update their registration, or aren&#8217;t sure how to register. National Voter Registration Day wants to make sure everyone has the opportunity to vote and seeks to create broad awareness of voter registration opportunities to reach tens of thousands of voters who may not register otherwise.</p>
<p>Be a changemaker Broncos and register to vote!</p>
