        if ($(this).attr("aria-expanded") === "false") {

        }

        toggleDropdown(nestedDropdownMenu);

    function toggleDropdown(menu) {
        if (menu.hasClass("hidden")) {
            menu.removeClass("hidden");
            menu.addClass("show");
            
            //add click listener to check for clicks outside of the nav
            //https://stackoverflow.com/questions/152975/how-do-i-detect-a-click-outside-an-element/3028037#3028037
            $(document).click(function(event) { 
                var $target = $(event.target);
                if(!$target.closest('#subNavContainer').length) {
                    closeDropdown(menu);
                }
            });
            
            //add listener for escape key
            $(document).on('keydown', function(event) {
                if (event.key == "Escape") {
                    closeDropdown(menu);
            }});
        } else {
            menu.removeClass("show");
            menu.addClass("hidden");
        }
    }

    function closeDropdown(menu) {
        var dropdownMenu = $("#dropdownMenu");
        var nestedDropdownMenus = $(".sub-nav__nested-dropdown");  
        $(".button").attr("data-dropdown-id", "dropdownMenuBtn").attr("aria-expanded", "false");

                $(".button").attr("data-dropdown-id", "dropdownMenuBtn").removeClass("show");
                $(".button").attr("data-dropdown-id", "dropdownMenuBtn").addClass("hidden");
            }
        })
        menu.removeClass("show");
        menu.addClass("hidden");
    }

                                    <button aria-expanded="false" aria-label="CSU Summer Arts Scholarships" class="accordion__header" tabindex="0">
                                        <h3 class="h6__proxima">CSU Summer Arts Scholarships</h3>
                                        <div class="accordion__item--right">
                                            <svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" class="accordion-chevron" fill="none" height="17" viewBox="0 0 29 17" width="29">

                                    <div class="accordion__content" tabindex="0">
                                        <p class="body2"><p>Students participating in the&#160;CSU Summer Arts Program can&#160;apply to have their fees covered while studying art, music, dance, media, theatre or writing next summer.</p>
<p>CSU Summer Arts scholarships are available to cover part or all of a student&#8217;s enrollment fees. Scholarships are rarely&#160;available to cover housing in addition to enrollment fees. There is only one scholarship application form, and all applicants will be considered for the maximum amount of aid available to them.</p>
<p>Scholarships are awarded on the basis of both financial need and talent. A complete&#160;application, including a written statement, will ensure you receive thorough consideration for aid.</p>
<p>The information below pertains to the application cycle for Summer 2023.&#160; Details for Summer 2024 will be posted in early Spring 2024.</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>How to Apply (Summer 2024)<br/></strong></span></h3>
<span style="font-size: 12pt;">To apply for a scholarship follow the instructions for applying for a course <strong>and</strong>:</span>
<ol>
<li>Prepare the materials listed below (scholarship statement and financial documentation) to submit at the time you submit your registration application. If you've already completed your registration application and would like to submit a scholarship application at a later time, contact the CSU&#160;Summer Arts office for instructions.</li>
<li>Compose a short (100- to 500-word) statement about&#160;why you are applying for scholarship assistance.</li>
<li>Prepare and scan your financial documentation (see below).</li>
<li>Complete and submit the online scholarship application, which will be available to you immediately upon completing your registration application (Upon completion of the registration application, you will be prompted to click through if you wish to complete a scholarship application.)</li>
</ol>
<h4><span style="font-size: 12pt;"><strong>Financial documentation consists of at least one of the items below</strong></span></h4>
<p>The&#160;questionnaire below will indicate which documentation you must submit:</p>
<ol>
<li>A copy of your 2022 federal tax return (Form 1040). We only need the first&#160;page of your tax return, indicating your adjusted gross income.</li>
<li>A copy of your parents&#8217; 2022 federal tax return (minors or dependents only). We only need the first&#160;page of your parents&#8217; tax return, indicating their adjusted gross income.</li>
<li>If you or your parents&#160;have not filed your 2022 federal income taxes, enclose copies of your W-2 or 1099 earning statements for 2022.</li>
</ol>
<h4><strong><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Whose financial documentation do you need to submit?</span> </strong></h4>
<p>We need <strong>your</strong>&#160;financial documentation if you are:</p>
<ul>
<li>born before January 1, 1999, or</li>
<li>a veteran of the U.S. Armed Forces, or</li>
<li>an orphan or ward of the court, or</li>
<li>have legal dependents other than a spouse, or</li>
<li>a graduate student, or</li>
<li>married</li>
</ul>
<p>If none of the above apply to you, we need <strong>your parents'</strong>&#160;financial documentation.</p>
<p>When you apply online, you will be required to complete a questionnaire listing the items above. You will then be prompted to submit only&#160;the documentation that applies to you. If you upload your personal information when you are required to submit your parents&#8217; information, you will not be considered for aid.</p>
<p><strong>If you don&#8217;t have any financial documentation</strong>, contact&#160;Joanne Sharp (<a href="mailto:jsharp@calstate.edu">jsharp@calstate.edu</a>) at the CSU Summer Arts office to&#160;work with you to provide acceptable paperwork.</p>
<p>Incomplete scholarship applications, or applications submitted without financial documentation and a written statement, will not be considered for scholarship assistance.</p>
<p>In most cases, you will be notified of your scholarship award status within two weeks of the application deadline for a course.</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Additional Information&#8203;</strong></span></h3>
<p>Financial aid through FAFSA and other programs is rarely applicable to CSU Summer Arts. Check with your home campus for availability. If you are a CSU student receiving financial aid, ask your campus financial aid office if you qualify for "visiting student status" to Fresno State. If you qualify, you will be able to apply your existing financial aid to CSU Summer Arts.</p>
<h3><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Scholarship Application</strong></span></h3>
<p>Ready to apply for a scholarship? <a href="https://www2.calstate.edu/SummerArts/Courses/Pages/registration.aspx">Click here</a> to start your registration application. When you complete your registration application, you will be prompted to complete a scholarship application.</p>
If you have already applied for registration and subsequently wish to submit a scholarship application, please contact the <a href="mailto:jsharp@calstate.edu">Summer Arts office</a> for assistance.</p>

                                    <button aria-expanded="false" aria-label="California Pre-Doctoral Program (Sally Casanova Scholars)" class="accordion__header" tabindex="0">
                                        <h3 class="h6__proxima">California Pre-Doctoral Program (Sally Casanova Scholars)</h3>
                                        <div class="accordion__item--right">
                                            <svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" class="accordion-chevron" fill="none" height="17" viewBox="0 0 29 17" width="29">

                                    <div class="accordion__content" tabindex="0">
                                        <p class="body2"><p>The California Pre-Doctoral Program is designed to increase the diversity of the pool from which the California State University draws its faculty. It does so by supporting the doctoral aspirations of CSU students who have experienced economic and educational disadvantages. A special emphasis is placed on increasing the number of CSU students who enter doctor of philosophy (Ph.D.) programs at one of the University of California campuses.</p>
<p>Each of the applicants selected will be designated a <strong>Sally Casanova Pre-Doctoral Scholar</strong> and will work closely with a CSU faculty mentor to develop an overall plan that leads to enrollment in a doctoral program. The plan should be tailored to the specific goals and career objectives of the student.</p>
<p>In addition the program provides:</p>
<ul>
<li>Travel Funds for the student to visit U.S. doctoral-granting institutions and/or to attend professional meetings appropriate to the student's development.</li>
<li>Development Funds for other related activities, such as student membership in professional organizations, subscriptions to academic journals, graduate school applications and test fees, GRE preparation, and the cost of minor research materials, for example.</li>
<li>A Summer Research Experience opportunity at a University of California campus or other U.S. major research university, fully funded by the Pre-Doctoral Program, so that the scholar can participate in doctoral-level research prior to applying to a Ph.D. program.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Eligibility</strong></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>Applicants must be upper-division or master's degree students who are enrolled at a CSU institution and, at the time of award, will be enrolled at a CSU campus for at least one semester or two (2) quarter terms in the academic year.</li>
<li>Each applicant must have a facult sponsor who will be available for the duration of the plan specified in the application.</li>
<li>The successful applicant must be a U.S. Citizen or Permanent Resident</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Not Eligible:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>International Students</li>
<li>Current or previous California Pre-Doctoral Scholars</li>
</ul>
Students interested in obtaining professional masters or doctoral degrees (law, medicine, dentistry, pharmacy, or an MBA degree, for example) are not eligible.<br/>
<h3><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Application and Deadlines</strong></span></h3>
<p><strong>The 2024-2025 application cycle opens in December 2023. </strong>The application and information on how to apply is available on the <a href="https://www.calstate.edu/csu-system/faculty-staff/predoc">CSU Chancellor's website</a>.&#160;</p>
<p>If you are interested in applying, you must immediately contact the Cal Poly Pomona Pre-Doctoral Coordinator for further information.</p>

<p>Dr. Claudia Garcia-Des Lauriers, Associate Professor &#8211; Geography and Anthropology</p>
<p>(909) 869-5264</p>
<p>Mr. Won Choi, Academic Coordinator of The Kellogg Honors College</p>
(909) 869-3209</p>

It provides fellowships for economically disadvantaged CSU students who have had success in overcoming educational disadvantages and promotes faculty mentoring and research opportunities. To apply for a Graduate Equity Fellowship, you must:</p>

<li>Enroll at least half-time in an eligible graduate&#160;degree; and</li>
</ul>
