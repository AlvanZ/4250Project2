            menu.removeClass("hidden");
            menu.addClass("show");

                if(!$target.closest('#subNavContainer').length) {
                    closeDropdown(menu);
                }
            });

Federal and state regulations require the coordination of all financial aid awards with all resources, including internal and external (private) scholarships and grants, fellowships, stipends, fee waivers (including the Cal-Vet fee waiver), and vocational rehabilitation and other educational benefits.</p>
<p>The total amount of assistance from all sources cannot exceed the Cal Poly Pomona yearly Cost of Attendance (COA). In addition, almost all federal, state, and Cal Poly Pomona grant programs require unmet financial need.
