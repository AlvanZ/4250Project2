				<h4><span style="font-size:20px;"><a href="https://www.cpp.edu/library/access-services/tech-lending-program.shtml" target="_self"><strong><span style="color:#006400;">Tech Lending and Support</span></strong></a></span></h4>

<p style="font-size:16px;font-weight:bold;">On-site Services</p>

<p>The Main Circulation Desk will be staffed to assist with checking out books and reserve materials and answering general questions and inquiries.</p>

<p>Individuals may also request items to be delivered to the 24/7 Bronco Lockers &ndash; <a href="https://www.cpp.edu/lockers/">https://www.cpp.edu/lockers/</a> &ndash; if they prefer contactless delivery OR cannot get to the Library while the Library is open.</p>

<p>Reference and research consultations will be available with librarians &ndash; either in person or virtually. Individuals can make an appointment in advance with the appropriate subject librarian &ndash; <a href="https://www.cpp.edu/library/reference-instruction/contact-subject-librarian.shtml">https://www.cpp.edu/library/reference-instruction/contact-subject-librarian.shtml</a>.</p>

<p>Individuals wishing to use Special Collections and Archives are required to make an appointment in advance. For more information, visit our website at: <a href="https://www.cpp.edu/library/special-collections/index.shtml">https://www.cpp.edu/library/special-collections/index.shtml</a>.</p>
