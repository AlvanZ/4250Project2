                                            <p>A tradition started by W.K. Kellogg in 1926, the Sunday Show features Cal Poly Pomona students riding Arabians in different disciplines from Native Costume to Ladies Side Saddle. Additionally, the trick horse act is always a crowd favorite. Training and handling are accomplished entirely by the W.K. Kellogg Arabian Horse Center's students and staff, and the show program is designed to demonstrate the versatility and fine qualities of the Arabian breed.</p>
<p>Weather permitting, the Sunday Show's are held on the first Sunday of the month at 2:00 PM. Shows are held in an outdoor arena, so rain any time the week before may affect scheduling. Please check this website or our <a href="https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100037191025956" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Facebook page</a> for updates. For the safety of the students and horses, pets (except service animals) are not allowed.</p>
